My work-study job is to teach computer science topics to K-12th grade students through CS@SC. The classes that I’ve taught include Scratch, Java, Python, and Game Design using Unity.

I love using technology to come up with creative solutions that can make a real difference in people’s lives—whether it’s through teaching others or personal projects.

On the side, I like playing basketball, weightlifting, and trying new food.
